Do you want to make a difference?

How you can help

It's easy to think about the injustices in the world and want to do something to help. But it's not enough to just think about it — to actually make a difference, we need to DO something. We need to take action.

Choose to make a difference in the life of a Mayan child by sponsoring for as little as $1 a day.

To sponsor a single child through a year of school – Preschool through High School – it costs $360/year. You may also choose to pay this as a monthly amount.

To sponsor a university student, it costs $1,350/year and this can also be paid monthly. There are also opportunities to co-sponsor a student for $675/year.

If you would rather have less of a commitment, you could aways donate however much you want, whenever you want, to Mayan Families.
